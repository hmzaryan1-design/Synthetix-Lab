Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 — Compliant with Moroccan Law 09-08 on Personal Data Protection.

1. Who We Are

Synthetix Lab ("we", "our", "us") is a digital marketing and AI automation agency based in Casablanca, Morocco. Contact: hello@synthetixlab.io | +212 699 121 342.

2. Data We Collect

When you contact us via our website form, we collect: your name, email address, company name, monthly budget range, and project details. We also collect basic analytics data (page views, browser type) through Google Analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to: respond to your enquiry, provide you with a project proposal, and communicate about our services. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact information for up to 3 years from the date of last contact. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

5. Your Rights (Law 09-08)

Under Moroccan Law 09-08 on the Protection of Personal Data, you have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, and object to data processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@synthetixlab.io.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand site traffic. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree for form submission and Google Analytics for site analytics. These services have their own privacy policies and data processing terms.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: hello@synthetixlab.io | Synthetix Lab, Casablanca, Morocco.
